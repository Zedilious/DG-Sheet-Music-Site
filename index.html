God Bless Our Church

God bless our church and all who pray within,
Bless us O Lord and keep us free from sin.
God Bless all those we love, all creatures great and small
Lord guide our weary footsteps, lest we should fall.


God bless our church our fellowship of love,
Thank thee O Lord for gifts sent from above.
Lord grant us every day good Christians we shall be,
With thankful hearts we lift our voices unto thee.


God bless our church help us to know your love,
Our guardian angels guide us from above.
Stay in our hearts and minds as we rejoice and sing,
Praises to thee dear Lord and father heavenly king.


Walk at our side Lord each and every day.
Dwell in our hearts and hear us when we pray.
Be thou our strength, O Lord our guide and saviour be.
Then in Gods heaven to dwell, for evermore with thee.


God bless our church and fill us with your grace.
Guide and protect us with your warm embrace.
Let all creation lift their voices now and sing
Praises and alleluias to our heavenly King.

Rejoice This Happy Morn

Rejoice this happy morn
to see the fields of golden corn,
our God is good indeed
for making grow the tiny seed.
He sends the rain to swell the grain
that fresh green shoots appear again,
Rejoice this happy morn
to see the fields of golden corn.


God sends the summer sun
the time for harvest has begun,
The trees bear fruits so sweet
so many fruits for us to eat.
Give thanks to God on this fair morn
for giving us the golden corn.
God sends the summer sun
the time for harvest has begun,


With blue skies overhead
the Lord sees that we all are fed.
The sheaves of corn appear
now is the special time of year.
The weary farmer rests at last
from hours of toil now day is past.
Give thanks to God our king
now all is safely gathered in.

Lord Of The Seasons

Lord of the seasons, Lord of the spring
With thankful Hearts we praise our God and King.
Mares with their foals in fields so fresh and green,
Our hearts are filled with joy, such wonders to be seen.

Blossoms of springtime, scenting the air,
Woodlands are wakening, new life everywhere.
Bird song at daybreak, new born lambs at play,
Beauty of Gods creation starts each glorious day.

Lord of the summer, sunshine so bright,
blue skies above us, give Gods heavenly light.
Bowers of roses, tender lilies fair,

Birds circle over head their sweet songs fill the air.
Fruits of the garden,corn turns to gold,
Gods hand at work ,his wonders to behold.
Flowers of summer colours bright and gay,
Beauty of nature, greet us each and every day.

Lord of the autumn, harvest is near,
Leaves turn to gold, red berries bright appear.
Nuts on the tree for squirrels winter store
Dormouse and tiny creatures, for us to adore.

Ripe corn is gathered now from the field
This precious harvest, for us now to yield
Fruits from the orchard, ripe fruits from the vine,
Thank thee dear Lord, for all these precious gifts of thine.

Lord of the winter, cold days are here.
Snow covered landscape at this time of year.,
Nature lies sleeping, bare trees looking sad
This is the day the Lord hath made rejoice and be glad

Christians awake now, angels on high,
Birth of a tiny babe is drawing nigh.
Triumphant songs, sweet choirs of angels sing.
Our saviour Jesus Christ is born, our God and King.
